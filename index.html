Chicago Taxi Trips

Project 3

CS 424 - Visualization and Visual Analytics

Authors: Swetha Jayakumar, Nihal Chandra

This projects aims to explore the Chicago Taxi Trips Dataset through interactive and informative Visualizations. The following questions have explored through Visualizations:

  • Question 1: How does the Trip Fare compare with the Trip Total for the entire week?
  • Question 2: Is the Trip Fare positively correlated to the Tip?
  • Question 3:How does the fare relate to the trip distance and duration?
  • Question 4:For each community area how do the top three taxi companies perform over the week?
